About This Experience

You've inherited a hotel room from a distant relative. People keep showing up at your door. Each has their own story about what happened here, about the woman who used to own this place. None of the stories quite match.

Look through the peephole. Decide whether to open the door. What you hear depends on who you ask.

Note: This experience requires a microphone. You will speak with the characters using your voice.
